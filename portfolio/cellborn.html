Cellborn

Synopsis

Made for a university Game Development module, Cellborn sees you as the intrepid Elfin trying to escape the evil Wizard's tower initially armed with nothing but a large collection of pebbles! Along the way, Elfin collects a wide variety of strange and interesting guns that can be used to defeat the fearsome fairies and spiteful spitting snakes that inhabit the tower!

Are you fearless enough to defeat the evil Wizard and earn your freedom?

Features

  • Completely procedurally generated dungeons
  • 13 unique weapons, some regular guns and some with their own special abilities:
    • Banana Bren - A Bren light machine gun that shoots whole bananas
    • BHG3000 - A gun that shoots a black hole that will suck up any enemies unfortunate enough to come too close
    • Bouncy Ball Bazooka - A bazooka that will fire a barrage of bouncy balls at your enemies
    • Deagle - A standard Desert Eagle pistol
    • Dualzi SMG - An Uzi 9mm named Daisy
    • F11 - A standard box rifle
    • Grappling Crossbow - A crossbow that will fire a grappling hook which pulls you along when it lands
    • LBR - A laser beam rifle which fires a continual stream of energy, blasting any and all enemies in its path
    • Murf Gun - A sniper rifle that fires freshly baked muffins that are irresistible to monsters. In this dungeon, it's murf or muffin
    • Music Gun - A boom box with an added gun attachment, firing bullets through the power of disco music
    • Pebble Bag - The default and seemingly endless bag of pebbles you start out with
    • Queen's Gambit - A white gun that fires a spread of clubs
    • Triple Barrel Shotgun - A normal shotgun with the added advantage of a third barrel
  • Two unique monsters:
    • Snakes that spit acid
    • Fairies that shoot balls of magic
  • An elements system:
    • Fire, ice, and electricity
    • The monsters and weapons have a randomly assigned elemental trait, with weapons doing more damage to monsters of the same elemental type
