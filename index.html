Recent Projects

Cellborn (June 2022)

Made for my final year university game development module, Cellborn is a top-down, isometric fantasy shooter where you play as an elf named Elvin, questing to escape the Evil Wizard's tower. Cellborn was made in Unity and is a collaborative effort between four programmers and four artists, and features procedural generation and unique weapons. My responsibilities during this project were the development of the weapons system, the potions, the HUD, the main menu, the settings menu, the introduction cutscene and the composition and implementation of the music.
